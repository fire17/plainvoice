How is this different from the free invoice generators I can Google?

Free web generators run on someone else's server. To use them you sign up, and the invoice details you type — client names, addresses, amounts — travel to and are stored by that company. Many watermark your PDF or cap you at a few invoices before a monthly fee. Plainvoice runs entirely inside your own browser from a single file on your disk. There is no server to send data to, no account, no watermark, and no recurring charge.

Where is my data stored?

On your machine only. Plainvoice keeps your current work in your browser and lets you export a small backup file (.plainvoice.json) that you own and control. You can back it up, move it between computers, or delete it — it's your file. Nothing is ever uploaded.

Do I need to install anything or be online?

No. Double-click the file and it opens in your browser. It works with the internet completely off. Nothing to install, no dependencies, no updates forced on you.

How do I get a PDF to send my client?

Click Export PDF. Plainvoice uses your browser's built-in print-to-PDF, so the result is crisp, has selectable text, and contains only the invoice — no app buttons, no watermark. Save it, email it, done.

Can I use my own logo and currency?

Yes. Add your logo, choose your accent colour, and pick from 14 currencies with correct formatting and any tax label you need (VAT, GST, sales tax, or none).
